Welcome!

Thanks for visiting my page!

I develop and apply tools to study the wiring (structural connections) of the brain using data acquired from diffusion magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). Neurological and psychiatric disorders may be related with changes to these connections. Primarily, my research is focused on the investigation of short-ranged, U-shaped connections (superficial white matter), which lie just below the cortical surface. These short-ranged connections comprise a majority of the connections found in the brain and are some of the last to mature. As a result, the U-shaped connections may be susceptible to abnormal development. I am also a supporter of open science practices and education.
